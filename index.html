Local art.
Local causes.

We are a Harrisburg-based nonprofit generating revenue through the sale of art donated by local artists. These donations go to charities supporting people-of-color, bail funds, and growth in our community.

Create.

Artists and craftsmen donate their wares to be sold by art4auction717. Buyers submit their bids, the highest winning the one-of-a-kind piece of artwork direct from the source.

Donate.

100% of the proceeds from auctions are donated to local charities. The charities are chosen based on their track-record, relevance to the community, and support of POC-backed causes.
